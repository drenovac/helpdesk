Employee is not being paid at the rate we expect

Employee 0486 Johnny Cash- as you can see by his payslip on the 4 and 5/12 he worked 14.5 hours however, it paid him for only 14 hours each day, we are at a loss to understand why the system did that.  (We have had a few of those recently with other staff)

Technote index


Introduction

To resolve this, we'll go straight to the employee rates details to confirm his pay class (classification code)

 

The employee is on CA-L1 CAS - the 14 base hours is indicating the level at which other considerations such as overtime kick in.
So, the next step is to open the Award Rule

 

The award rule as defined below does nothing with any hours post the base hours in the day - and hence the reason why the payments max out at 14.00

 

 

 

Resolution

Depending up how you actually wish to deal with this, you have 2 options

  • Change the award rule to do something with the hours over 14
  • Change the base hours per shift to 23 - which will then pay up to 23 hours per day without further reference to the award rule.
