Introduction

You have a national contract to provide services, and have chosen to sub-contract the work through to one of your sub-contractors. There are a number of goals in this setup:

  • Manage the time & attendance of the sub-contractor
  • Manage the invoicing of your client
  • Ensure a profit margin between the charges you impose on your client vs the fees you pay to your sub-contractor
