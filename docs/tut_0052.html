Issue

Commencing 01/07/2013 the Employer Superannuation contribution rate increases at a rate of 0.25% / annum. To make the configuration change in Powerforce use the Superfund control window (on System Controls / Payroll / Superannuation menu).

For each fund requiring the change, open the fund record, and:

  • Enter the new rate (e.g. 9.25) in the Rate field as shown.
    Enter the effective date of the rate change in the Date field
    Press"Apply" to commit your change to the database.

Using this approach, you may enter the new Superannuation rates for future years at the same time as you enter the 2013 (as per the above example) details.

To confirm that you change has worked, re-open the saved record..
