Having to edit pay - won't pick up site rate

Customer is reporting that they're having to manually override pay rates when employees work at specific site, because the system is not calculating it automatically.

See Also

Technote index

Overview

The client / employee rate override is configured through the 'client operations' screen.

and, you will need to confirm the 'grade' of the employee's classification (below)

and, you will need to verify the employment type for the employee, which in this case is "FULLTIME" (below)


Resolution

In this instance a couple of items are not configured correctly & subsequently causing the percieved issue.

  • The employee is 'fulltime' and therefore you must configure the opposite side of the "Auto Classification" for the site
    The employee pay class 'grade' is 1, not "CA-L1 M-S PPT" (this is the pay class id)
