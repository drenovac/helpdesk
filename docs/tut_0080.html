Standard schedule display showing incorrect shifts

I am trying to set up a rotation for a client 542 London.

The shifts for this site are only Monday to Friday however when I enter the "Maintain Standard Schedule to build the rotation it is has moved the shifts to Sundays as well and had put Friday and Saturday without shifts instead of Saturday and Sunday without shifts.

There does not seem to be any logical reason for this that I can work out could someone have a look at this please and advise??

I am setting up a 6 week rotation.
  • There is no current rotation set up 
  • I was unable to set up rotation due to the display being incorrect
  • If you have fixed the way it is displaying that is ALL I need as I can now go ahead and make the rotation

This image demonstrates the error in the client standard schedule display, with the 'Sun' settings, which should not be there.

 

Introduction

To resolve the issue, we'll investigate the overall client work schedules through to client standard schedules workflow.

Printing the report format of this is the quickest path to view the overall work requirements. The image is showing that there are no weekend shifts defined.

We need to confirm the 09/03/15 date to confirm the day of the week.
To achieve this, (1) press the Edit Setting button to open the form to editing, and then (2) dropdown on the date to obtain a calendar view of the date.

 

 

 

Resolution

Scheduling configuration in Powerforce starts Monday and finishes Sunday (irrespective of your scheduling week) for the purposes of configuration.

Change the week ending date to a Sunday, and the display corrects itself.
