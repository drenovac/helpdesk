Accounts not to invoice a customer, until 2nd job is done

Operations are running a specific event for a client, and wish to ensure that accounts do not bill the client until the 2nd event is completed.

The regular billing is happening on this coming Monday, however, the clients 2nd event is being held on the Tuesday.
Operations want to know if there's an internal mechanism other than email through which they can ensure that accounts do not bill the client, until the 2nd event is complete.

Overview

This is an interesting proposition - our initial response was that there is not anything that Operations can control to stop accounts invoicing ahead of the 2nd event, other than person to person communication.

However, as it turns out there is a 'trick' through which Operations can control / ensure that the client is not billed.

Accounts billing works on the mechanism of 'billing cycle' - and accounts generally run billing runs around the billing cycle (as indicated below)

XXX (image goes here)

If you create a billing cycle called "operations on hold" - operations can then change the billing cycle on the client record, and accounts will not accidentally run invoicing for that client.
