Change Roster Order #

Often times users will forward schedule for months at a time.
Included in that scheduling is the Customer Purchase Order number, and as happens the Purchase Order changes over time.

For the occassions where you have this scenario, we have developed a quick tool which allows you to programatically alter the old to the new purchase order #.

Technote index

You'll find the new option under 'Scheduling' in your menus


Once the process has run, you will be given a brief outline so that you may double check that data (if any) has changed.
