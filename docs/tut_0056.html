In the NSW database sick leave is paying evening and weekend loadings. See below.  
Can you please advise how to correct this? 
Or is this in the background?

Overview

An employee is rostered to two shifts on a weekend (Sat & Sun).

The employee calls in sick, so operations alter the shift status to 'Sick Leave' (as shown below).

However, the award calculations are incorrect, with the user indicating that sick leave is paid at normal hours only and not as per the calculations shown in the images.




The 'Sick Leave' is known as a scheduling code in Powerforce rostering parlance, and hence is driven by a control record.

The details of the "Sick Leave" scheduling code for this database, are as below. The two checks to define the payment of sick leave are the "Pay Normal Hours" and "Pay Sick Leave" check-boxes.

However, we notice in this setting that the "Apply Award" is also checked. Since, the intent is to pay normal hours only, you need to un-check this flag.

Un-checking the Apply Award flag, saving the record, and re-costing the two shifts in question bring the data into line with the expected award behaviour.
