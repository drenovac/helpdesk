In the example below the public holiday loading is not showing.  I’ve gone through and had a look and it appears that it’s not picking up the loading on the CLEAN classification

Explanation

Using the scheduling workbench, locate and open the shift. Confirm on the times tab that the classification is CLEAN, then check the "Costs" tab, and indeed there is no PHOL (Public Holiday) loading.

Check the calendar to confirm that the date 28/01/13 is indeed marked as a public holiday, and in this example it is.

Check the details of the classification code, verify the award interpreter definitions. Verify as per the marking below that on the Public Holiday column, that you are paying "N"ormal hours & "PHL" (link) loading.


Open the Penalties tab of the classification, and firstly check the lower section where the PHL (link from the Award Interpreter) is correctly stating to apply an Allowance "PHL" at 37.1429 loading atop of the normal hours. So, that is correct.

However, as the arrow indicates - the classification does not "Pay Public Holiday" and hence this is the reason why no public holiday loading is being applied.

Check the "Pay Public Holiday" checkbox and re-cost the shift and you will have the award working as expected.
