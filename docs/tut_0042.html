Can you please tell me how I can remove the overheads on all of the costs associated with the labour.  I just want their hours worked multiplated by their rate to give the cost.  I need to make sure this is done on all costs for each venue

See Also

Helpdesk Index pre-2013 technotes

Explanation

 


The overheads refered to as those on the Costs tab of the edit shift window (Scheduling workbench).


The parameters shown above are controlled / configured via the Company Controls record (per company basis), and can be set to 0 to exclude the overheads from coming through.
